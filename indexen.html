Admission process

Follow the steps below — it takes a few minutes, and then we’ll help you further.

1

Choose a program

Pick your field and study format. If you’re not sure — call/message us, we’ll help.

2

Prepare documents

Collect the basic document package. Please confirm the exact list with the admissions office.

3

Submit an application

Apply online or submit documents in person. After that, we’ll contact you to confirm.

4

Confirmation & enrollment

Get a response, sign the documents, and receive instructions to start studying.
